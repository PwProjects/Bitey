# Bitey

Bitey is Factorio mod that introduces a pet biter companion to the game. Bitey will follow you, assist in combat, react to the world, grow over time, and express its personality through emotes.

---

## Features

- **Companion** - Bitey follows the player, assists in combat, and stays close during exploration.
- **Needs** - Bitey has several biological and emotional needs that the player will have to navigate.
- **Growth** — Bitey grows over time, animations, and rare events.
- **Interactions** — Bitey reacts to player actions, environmental events, structures, items and combat situations with expressive emotes.

---

## Installation

1. Download the latest release ZIP from the **Releases** page.
2. Place the ZIP file into your Factorio mods directory:

   **Windows:**  
   `%APPDATA%\Factorio\mods`

   **Linux:**  
   `~/.factorio/mods`

   **macOS:**  
   `~/Library/Application Support/factorio/mods`

3. Launch Factorio and enable the mod in the Mods menu.

---

## Compatibility

- Compatible with **Factorio 2.0+**.
- Works in both single‑player and multiplayer.
- Should be compatible with most mods that do not alter core unit AI or player-following behavior.
- If you encounter conflicts, please open an issue.

---

## Configuration

The mod includes several startup and runtime settings:

- **Pet Behavior Settings** — Adjust follow distance, aggression, or idle behaviors.
- **Visual Settings** — Toggle glow effects, emotes, and memorial visuals.
- **Performance Settings** — Control update frequency for lower‑end systems.

All settings can be found under:  
**Options → Mods → Biter Pet**

---

## Known Issues

- Pets may occasionally get stuck on complex terrain layouts; they will teleport to the player if separated for too long.
- Some rare emote sequences may overlap during heavy combat; this will be refined in future updates.
